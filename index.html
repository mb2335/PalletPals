INFO 498 Capstone: Village Vision
On the first business day of each month, our primary contact at each village receives an automated notification (text? email?) requesting that they update their impact report. They answer the same five-six questions every month. That data is collected and stored. Pallet will be able to see a single village performance, as well as the aggregated data/impact of all villages.
